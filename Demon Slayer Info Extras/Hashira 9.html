Mitsuri Kanroji

Mitsuri Kanroji is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. She is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the current Love Hashira . Mitsuri is a very emotional, passionate, and jovial individual who constantly compliments people in her head, likely a contributing factor towards her nickname as the Hashira of Love. Although she can be shy and is easily flustered, Mitsuri is always kind to others.
