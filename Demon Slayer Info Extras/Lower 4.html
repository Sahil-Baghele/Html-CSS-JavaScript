Mukago

Mukago was a member of the Twelve Kizuki, having held the position of Lower Rank Four . Like most demons, Mukago held great fear and respect for Muzan Kibutsuji. However, out of all the Lower Ranks, she was also shown to be the most cowardly. As stated by Muzan through reading her thoughts, Mukago had no intention of battling the Demon Slayer Corps' Hashira and planned to flee at the mere mention of one, noted to have done so numerous times. When Muzan learned of this, Mukago attempted to deny her thoughts in an attempt to appease him.
