Tanjuro Kamado

Tanjuro Kamado a minor character of Demon Slayer : Kimetsu No Yaiba. He was the husband of Kie Kamado, and the father of Tanjiro, Nezuko, Takeo, Hanako, Shigeru, and Rokuta. Tanjuro was a flame dancer who used to dance the Hinokami Kagura during the winter. Tanjuro was quiet, wise, and loving person. He had a loving relationship with his family and had a very close father-son bond with Tanjiro. Even when he was ill, he still danced the Hinokami Kagura to celebrate their family's tradition.
