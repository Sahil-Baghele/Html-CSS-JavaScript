Tengen Uzui

Tengen Uzui is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and a major character in the Entertainment District Arc. He was a former member of the Demon Slayer Corps, standing as the previous Sound Hashira. Tengen is an eccentric and flashy individual, always wanting to be flamboyant, possessing a need for everything to be flashy and outstanding, even proclaiming that he is the god of flashiness and festivals.
