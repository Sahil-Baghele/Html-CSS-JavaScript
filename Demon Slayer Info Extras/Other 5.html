Genya Shinazugawa

Genya Shinazugawa is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps and the younger brother of the Wind Hashira, Sanemi Shinazugawa. Genya was initially a foul-mouthed and ill-tempered individual; a lone wolf who refused any help from others and did not show any interest in being polite or having any manners.
