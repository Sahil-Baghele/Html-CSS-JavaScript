kie kamado

Kie Kamado is a minor character in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. She was the wife of Tanjuro Kamado and the mother of Tanjiro and Nezuko Kamado. She was killed by Muzan Kibutsuji. Kie was a warm and loving person; as the mother of 6 children, she was always seen displaying a very affectionate and caring personality.
