Sakonji Urokodaki

Sakonji Urokodaki is a retired member of the Demon Slayer Corps, having held the position of the previous Water Hashira . He is the main cultivator of the Water Breathing style, having trained Makomo, Sabito, Giyu Tomioka, and Tanjiro Kamado. As a trainer and former Demon Slayer, Sakonji is pragmatic, strict, and vigilant, while simultaneously sympathetic and understanding.
